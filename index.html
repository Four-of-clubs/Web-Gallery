About

What is This?

This is a Gallery of my personal projects!

Since 2021 I've been adding to a modest collection of these visual toys - things I catch myself referring to as “wannabe screensavers.” They are primarily built in LÖVE (also called LOVE2D) or PICO-8; both Lua-based game engines.

I have a few other projects that are more game-like, that is, interactive. This gallery is for the ones meant to be displayed not played, though if you are itching to see a game I've made check out Clyde's Adventure!

Who am I?

My name is Alex.

I'm a software developer by trade and passion, and I love yo-yos!
